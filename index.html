BUILDING FOUNDATIONS FOR LIFE-LONG SUCCESS

Evolving from its humble beginnings as a boutique pre-primary institution, Shalom Hills International School has grown into a distinguished CBSE-affiliated co-educational institution, consistently ranked among the top ten CBSE establishments in Gurugram.

Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, fuelled by a passion to make world-class education accessible to every learner. With a strong focus on innovation, values, and personalized learning, we continue to nurture confident, capable, and compassionate future leaders.

We firmly believe that excellence flourishes in an environment filled with warmth and inclusivity. Situated in the heart of Gurugram, our institution serves as a shining example of top-tier education, holistic development and a nurturing ambiance for learners.

Our motto - Peace, Progress and Prosperity, mirrors our core values. True to our name, SHALOM, which signifies PEACE, our motto embodies the vision of "World Peace Through Education."
